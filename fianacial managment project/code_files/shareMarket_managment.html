Market Value Ratios

Market value ratios help investors evaluate a company's stock price and financial performance.

  • P/E Ratio (Price-to-Earnings Ratio): Compares stock price to earnings per share.
  • P/S Ratio (Price-to-Sales Ratio): Compares stock price to revenue per share.

These ratios provide insights into valuation and investment decisions.




Market Share Ratio

The P/E Ratio (Price-to-Earnings Ratio) compares a company's stock price to its earnings per share (EPS).

Formula for P/E Ratio:

$$ \text{P/E Ratio} = \frac{\text{Stock Price}}{\text{Earnings per Share}} $$

Investors use this ratio to assess valuation and make informed investment decisions.
